Our Mission


At Sweet Hope, we believe that even small actions can make a big difference. After losing both a relative and a classmate to suicide, I realized just how serious this issue is. I knew I had to do something, so I started baking. Every cupcake and cookie we sell helps raise money for the Central Minnesota Mental Health Center, supporting suicide prevention and mental health resources for teens.


We want to raise awareness, start conversations, and let people know they are not alone. My goal is to bring hope to those who need it and help create a world where no teen feels like giving up. Through kindness, awareness, and community, we are committed to making a difference one sweet treat at a time. Because sometimes, something as small as a cookie can be the first step toward something bigger. Learn more on the Donate page.
